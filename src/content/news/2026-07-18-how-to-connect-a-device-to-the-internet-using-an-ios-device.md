Use this guide to connect a device to the internet with Personal Hotspot on an iOS device.

## Before You Begin

1. Plug in and power on the device.
2. Verify that the blue indicator light on the device is illuminated. This confirms that the device is powered on.
3. Once the device is powered on, follow the steps below.

## Connect With Personal Hotspot

1. Open **Settings** on the iOS device.
2. Tap **General**.
3. Tap **About**.
4. Tap **Name**.
5. Change the device name to **ENS**, then tap **Done**.
6. Turn **Cellular Data** on.
7. Return to **Settings**, then tap **Personal Hotspot**.
8. Turn on **Allow Others to Join**, then tap **Wi-Fi Password**.
9. Change the Wi-Fi password to **ensdevicego!**, then save your changes.
10. Wait a few minutes while the device connects to the hotspot.

## Confirm the Device Status

Sign in to your RunS.A.M. account and check the device status.

- **Green** means the device is online and has successfully connected.
- **Red** means the device is still offline. Wait another minute and refresh the page. If the status remains red, verify that the hotspot settings are configured correctly and that the hotspot is still enabled.

## Expected Result

The iOS device remains available as a Personal Hotspot, and the connected device appears online in RunS.A.M.
