## Before You Begin

1. Plug in and power on the S.A.M. Stick device.
2. Verify that the blue indicator light on the device is illuminated. This confirms that the device is powered on.
3. Once the device is powered on, follow the steps below.

## Instructions

1. Open the Settings app on your Android device.

2. Tap Connections.

3. Select Mobile Hotspot and Tethering.

4. Turn on Mobile Hotspot by tapping the toggle switch. Then tap Mobile Hotspot to configure the hotspot settings.

![Android Mobile Hotspot and Tethering screen with the Mobile Hotspot option highlighted](https://media.wwds.co/u/d8de8b28-02c9-4415-8ce6-9ce74a0e9c4d.webp)

5. Tap Configure.

![Android Mobile Hotspot screen with the Configure button highlighted](https://media.wwds.co/u/1d791925-2647-4815-b981-2cff39f49611.webp)

6. Enter the following hotspot details:

   - Hotspot Name (SSID): `ENS`
   - Password: `ensdevicego!`

   Once the information has been entered, tap Save.

![Android Configure Mobile Hotspot screen showing network name ENS, password field, and Save button](https://media.wwds.co/u/71afee48-f8c4-4cd0-99c9-a2e221856a71.webp)

7. Verify that Mobile Hotspot is turned on. The S.A.M. Stick device should now be able to connect using the configured hotspot.

![Android Mobile Hotspot screen showing hotspot turned on with network name ENS](https://media.wwds.co/u/90de527b-c18e-4182-a40e-6692811c1692.webp)

8. Wait a few minutes while the device connects to the hotspot.

9. Sign in to your [RunSam.com](https://runsam.com) account and check the device status:

   - Green: the device is online and has successfully connected.
   - Red: the device is still offline. Wait another minute and refresh the page. If the status remains red, verify that the hotspot settings are configured correctly and that the hotspot is still enabled.

## Expected Result

The S.A.M. Stick device appears online in RunSam.com after it connects to the Android mobile hotspot.
